# BlockCoach Fair Play Policy

BlockCoach is a coaching and training tool. It is not a cheat client.

## Allowed product behavior

BlockCoach may:

- read safe client-side gameplay signals
- send local telemetry to the user's own Local Bridge
- summarize training progress
- create Daily Quests
- help the player review deaths, losses, habits, and improvement areas
- reward training consistency with XP, streaks, and quest progress

## Forbidden product behavior

BlockCoach must not:

- aim for the player
- click for the player
- attack for the player
- move the camera
- automate movement
- automate inventory decisions
- automate gameplay
- mark enemies through walls
- expose hidden information
- modify hitboxes
- change reach
- bypass anti-cheat systems
- inject tactical live advantages during a fight

## Coach boundary

The product should focus on learning:

```text
Play → observe → review → train better next time
```

The product should not become:

```text
Detect opponent → give unfair live advantage → automate action
```

## Server respect

Users are responsible for following the rules of the servers they play on. If a server forbids client-side telemetry mods, users should not use BlockCoach there.

## Release wording

Avoid marketing claims like:

```text
Win automatically.
Destroy everyone.
Get an unfair PvP edge.
```

Prefer:

```text
Learn from your fights.
Track your training.
Level up your skills.
```
